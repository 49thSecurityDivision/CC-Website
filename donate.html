Can I donate to CarolinaCon?

We will gladly accept donations from anyone who would like to contribute. At CarolinaCon, we pride ourselves on not charging a lot for admission. Because of this, we don't have a lot to spend on giveaways (we manage though). We can always use prizes for Hacker Trivia and various other contests that we run, so if you want to donate an actual prize, rather than cash, just let us know by sending an email to: info (at) carolinacon.org

To donate cash, please use the donation page for 49thsecuritydivision.
