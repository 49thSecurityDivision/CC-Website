CarolinaCon Online will be live from 7PM EST 4/23/21 to 11:45PM EST 4/25/21

To better share our NC pride with a larger audience this year we have chosen to theme the con after traditional NC BBQ! Winners in the CTF will be mailed some of our favorite local NC BBQ sauces!
